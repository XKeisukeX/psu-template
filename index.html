Pangasinan State University (Filipino: Pamantasang Pampamahalaan ng Pangasinan) also referred to by its acronym PSU or (PangSU)) is a public university in Pangasinan province, Philippines. The university was founded in its current form in 1979, although its origins trace back to the 1920s.
